Description

ABOUT THE CUBE REACTION HYBRID PERFORMANCE 500 ELECTRIC

Take the road less travelled, and see where it takes you.

The Reaction Hybrid Performance takes the power of Bosch's third generation system and adds sophisticated PowerTube design and easy-to-use Shimano 9-speed gears.

The XS and S sizes feature 27.5 inch wheels with the M, L, XL, and XXL sizes sporting the bigger 29 inch wheel size.

KEY FEATURES

A wider saddle and lock-on grips with a I 00m Suntour suspension fork.

Form follows function. The Reaction Hybrid Performance's elegant ensure you're comfortable for the long haul. Cube also fitted Shimano hydraulic disc brakes for safe, all-weather control. Follow your curiosity.

A Trapeze frame option gives riders who are more comfortable with a lower top tube an easy access option, without compromising performance. Plus, with Size Split, you can be assured of finding the perfect fit.
